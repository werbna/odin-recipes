Cheese Tortellini

Cheese tortellini is a delicious pasta that is perfect for a quick weeknight meal

This homemade cheese tortellini is made with simple ingredients

Ingredients:

  • Italian Sausage
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Cream Cheese
  • Cheese Tortellini
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Optional: Fresh Basil, Freshly Grated Parmesan Cheese, Garlic

Instructions

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat
  2. Add Italian sausage and cook until browned
  3. Stir in marinara sauce and cream cheese until melted
  4. Stir in cooked cheese tortellini
  5. Top with mozzarella cheese and bake until bubbly
  6. Optional: Garnish with fresh basil, freshly grated Parmesan cheese, and garlic
