Chinese Orange Chicken

Chinese orange chicken is a delicious dish that is perfect for a quick meal

This homemade orange chicken recipe is made using simple ingredients

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breast
  • Orange Juice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Orange Zest
  • Optional: Green Onions, Sesame Seeds

Instructions

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat
  2. Add chicken breast and cook until browned
  3. Stir in orange juice, soy sauce, garlic, and orange zest
  4. Simmer until sauce is thickened
  5. Optional: Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds
